What's Included in Every Plan

  • Unlimited Notes and Tasks
  • Rich text editor with file attachments
  • Task management (Table & Kanban views)
  • Custom task statuses
  • Team management and roles
  • Workflow automation (with plan limits)
  • Shopify admin extension (quick actions)

Free Trial

New users can try Starter or Growth for free for 15 days. Full access to all features — no charge until the trial ends. The trial is one-time only.

How to Upgrade

Go to Settings → Billing (or click Billing in the sidebar)
Review the plan comparison
Click Start Starter or Upgrade to Growth
Confirm through Shopify's billing system — new features are available immediately

How to Downgrade

Go to Billing and click Schedule Downgrade. The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle — you keep your current features until then.

⚠️
Before downgrading, review your active workflows. If you exceed the new plan's limits, some workflows may be paused.
