Your command center — get a quick snapshot of your store's health, team workload, and automation performance.

Prioritize risks and monitor store activity at a glance.

Three cards showing Checkout risk (Healthy), Fulfillment (Action — 1 overdue), and Inventory (Healthy).

Attention Required

Flags critical issues: open issue count, severity badge, and quick-access "Open issue center" button.

KPI Quick Stats

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see a row of KPI cards

KPI cards: Checkout risk (0, Healthy), Fulfillment (1 overdue — Action), Inventory (0, Healthy)
showing important store metrics:

KPIWhat It Means
🔔 Attention RequiredItems that need your immediate action
📦 Overdue FulfillmentOrders that should have been fulfilled but haven't
🛒 Abandoned CheckoutsCustomers who didn't complete their purchase
💰 Pending PaymentOrders with payment still pending
🚫 Out of StockProducts completely out of stock
⚠️ Low InventoryProducts running low on stock
KPI Card Row

Each card has an icon, count, colored badge (green "Healthy" or red "Action"), detail lines, and a quick-action link.

Click on any KPI card
Each card has a quick-action link: 'Open checkouts', 'Open orders', or 'Open inventory'
 to see more details or take action.

Charts

The dashboard includes visual charts to help you understand patterns:

Task Charts

  • Task Priority Distribution — Bar chart showing Low, Medium, High, and Urgent tasks
  • Task Status Breakdown — How many tasks are in each status
  • Staff Workload — How many tasks each team member has assigned
  • Completion Rate — Donut chart showing completed vs. open tasks

Workflow Charts

  • 7-Day Workflow Trends — Daily success vs. failure chart
  • Workflow Success Rate — Overall success rate over time
  • Failure Trends — Spot if a workflow is failing repeatedly
Open Tasks by Priority

Bar chart sorted by severity — Urgent (red, tallest), High (orange), Medium (yellow). Helps surface urgent work first.

Staff Workload

Table showing open task count per team member and the unassigned queue, with quick action links.

7-Day Workflow Trends

Daily success vs. failure bars from Apr 1–8 showing workflow execution volume ramping up.

Recent Activity

The Recent Activity feed at the bottom of the dashboard shows the latest team and automation events in real time.

Activity Log

Each row logs an action (task status update, workflow created), the user, and the time. Click "View all" for full history.

Upcoming Scheduled Workflows

See which scheduled workflows are queued to run next, along with their current status.

Upcoming Scheduled Workflows

Clickable workflow names with "Active" badges. "View all" link to manage all scheduled workflows.

Changing the Date Range

By default, the dashboard shows data from the last 7 days. To change this:

Go to Settings
Find Dashboard Stats Date Range
Choose from: Today, Last Week, Last Month, or Last 3 Months
Operational Summary

Snapshot of key metrics for the selected date range. Shows active workflows, open tasks, automation runs, and last update timestamp.
